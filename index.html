Shopify Theme App Extension

Category Tiles

Display beautiful, customizable subcategory tiles on your Shopify collection pages — no code required.

Setup Guide

Get started in under 5 minutes — no coding required.

1

Automatic creation of metafield

Immediately after installing the application, the Subcollections metafield is automatically created.

2

Create your Parent Collection

In the Shopify Admin, click on the field Products → Collections, then create your Parent Collection (e.g. Snowboards).

3

Create your Child Collections

Stay in the Collection tab and create your Child Collections (e.g. Freeride, Freestyle, Light).

4

Navigate to the Metafields section in your Parent Collection

Navigate to your Parent Collection (e.g. Snowboards), scroll down to the Metafields section and click on View all.

5

Add your Child Collections to your Parent Collection

Once you are on the Metafields page, click on the Subcollections metafield and select the Child Collections you want to add (e.g. Freestyle, Light, Freeride).

5

Add the app block to your theme

Navigate to Online Store → Themes → Edit theme. Select the page where you want to display your Category tiles. Then click Add section/Add block.

6

Customize the design to suit your needs

On the right side of Theme editor customize your Category tiles.
